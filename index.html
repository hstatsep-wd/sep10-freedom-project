Hardware

Tablets

Tablets are commonly used by digital artists as they are the most easiest hardware to use when it comes to making art. The most common ones are:

  • iPad & iPad Pro
  • Samsung S Tabs
  • Wacom & Huion
  • and much more!

Picture of Huion Tablet

Laptops/PCs

Laptops and PCs are also another option, as they come in a lot of varieties and can support a lot of drawing apps. Models of these laptops/PCs are:

  • Macbooks and Macs
  • Surface Laptops
  • and much more!

Picture of Surface Laptop Pro

Phones

While not used often, phones are a great option for artists who don't want to spend money, and they support art apps just as well as the previous hardware. Some phones are:

  • iPhone
  • Samsung Galaxy
  • and much more!

Picture of Galaxy s23 Ultra

Styluses

Many digital artists often use styluses as an easier way to draw on hardware. Some examples of these stylueses are:

  • Apple Pencil
  • S-Pen
  • Logitech Pencil
  • and much more!

Picture of Apple Pencil

Part B: Possible Future Inventions

There are many possibilities in the future on how art technology could advance, but here are some ideas of inventions of technology for art in the future. Click on the titles to see more!

Vr Art

  • Headset and a stylus are included (uses bluetooth)
    • Stylus can change brushes automatically with buttons, plus eraser
  • Shows screen with different programs to use for art
  • Can project your painting on a wall to see size

Touch Screen Projector

  • Can use projector on the floor or walls
  • Can use the screen of the projector
  • Can connect stylus with it, and stylus has different functions
    • Stylus has eraser, buttons to change brushes, motions to undo/redo, etc
  • Can project you artwork to see the size

Art Laptop

  • Free drawing programs
  • The screen can go to different angles
  • Has a stylus with it
    • Has eraser and buttons with multiple functions
  • Has dial that changes brushes
  • Can print out your artwork
