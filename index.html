Part B: Possible Future Inventions

There are many possibilities in the future on how art technology could advance, but here are some ideas of inventions of technology for art in the future. Click on the titles to see more!

Vr Art

  • Headset and a stylus are included (uses bluetooth and can change brushes automatically with buttons)
  • Shows screen with different programs to use for art
  • Can project your painting on a wall to see size
  • Is able to guide you and help you improve your art

Touch Screen Projector

  • Can use projector on the floor or walls
  • You can use the screen of the projector
  • You can connect stylus with it, and stylus has different functions, like changing brushes and gestures
  • You can project you artwork for people to see
  • Helps with making digital art be sawn by people in a real life situation, like paintings in museums, for example

Art Laptop

  • Free drawing programs
  • The screen can go to different angles
  • Has a stylus with it (which has an eraser and buttons with mulitiple functions)
  • Has dial that changes brushes
  • Can print out your artwork
  • Helps artists with a more portable way to make digital art and do extra things like put it on paper
